AICTE IDEA Lab
Rathinam Technical Campus

A hands-on innovation hub that transforms ideas into prototypes. Access 3D printers, CNC, laser cutters, electronics benches, and mentorship to build industry-ready projects and startup ventures.

Explore Focus Areas See Infrastructure

Focus Areas

Additive Manufacturing

3D printing, CNC routing and laser cutting to take designs from idea to functional prototype.

IoT & Smart Systems

Embedded systems, sensors, and cloud analytics for real-world automation and monitoring.

Entrepreneurship

Pre-incubation, mentorship, and industry linkages to help startups launch and scale.

Infrastructure & Tools

  • 3D Printers (FDM & SLA), CO₂ Laser Engraver, CNC Machines
  • Electronics benches, Oscilloscopes, Power Supplies, Microcontrollers
  • Welding & Fabrication, Vertical Drilling, Precision Tools
  • Design Room, Collaboration Hubs, 24x7 Lab Access

Programs & Activities

Structured AICTE training, workshops, ideathons, hackathons, interdisciplinary projects, and mentorship sessions to move students from concept to market-ready products.
